Test Engineer and aspiring developer

I went to UC Irvine where I received a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering in 2017. I'm currently working as a Test Engineer for AutoRoboto, at Google, where I'm assisting with the scaling effort to support AR capabilities on hundreds of millions of Android devices. Some of my biggest interests include Machine Learning, Visual Odometry and Computer Vision, Robotics, and Augmented Reality.
